Scratch Game

The Scratch Maze Game is an interactive game where players control an egg on its journey to find its way home to its mother. Along the way, the egg must navigate through a maze while avoiding obstacles such as snakes and rats. Upon reaching its destination, the egg hatches into a chick, symbolizing the successful completion of its journey and reunion with its mother. The game combines elements of problem-solving, strategy, and adventure, offering players an engaging and entertaining experience as they help the egg on its quest.

This is an experiment I have done when I was Learning KNES 373. In this experiment, we explored two major concepts: Exercise Efficency & Economy and VO2 max.

Efficiency = the frac8on of the energy produced that is translated into mechanical work.

Economy = the amount of oxygen needed to generate a given power or speed in steady state condi8ons.

Efficiency= [mechanical work (kcal) / energy input (kcal)] x 100

Economy = VO2 (mL/kg/min) / speed (km/min)

Maximal oxygen uptake (VO2max) is the highest rate of taking in and delivering oxygen to the working muscles. It represents the maximal volume of oxygen that can be used per minute at a maximum workload for a given exercise

Exercise Efficiency & Economy

The differences between effiency & econnomy is that exercise efficiency focuses on the overall energy utilization and work output during physical activity, while exercise economy examines the energy cost associated with maintaining a specific exercise intensity or speed.
